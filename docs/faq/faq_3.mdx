What are lootboxes, how do I earn them and what are the odds?

<Underline />

Lootboxes are a way to gain a little exp boost. 
You can earn Lootboxes as thanks by voting for the bot.<br />
You can open Lootboxes in every server that has the option to open them enabled.<br />
<br />
There are 4 different Lootboxes:<br />
<TitleImage image={"https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1040802605200330774/1061712822423277668/Brown3.png"} size={"24px"}></TitleImage>Common<br />
<TitleImage image={"https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1040802605200330774/1061712822746234950/Green3.png"} size={"24px"}></TitleImage>Uncommon<br />
<TitleImage image={"https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1040802605200330774/1061712823044018316/Red3.png"} size={"24px"}></TitleImage>Rare<br />
<TitleImage image={"https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1040802605200330774/1061712822100300006/Purple3.png"} size={"24px"}></TitleImage>Legendary<br />
<br />
The exp you get with each crate/rarity increases with your level.<br />

Vote streak counts as maximum 3%, each vote in streak counts as 0.1%.<br />
<br />
<TitleImage image={"https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1040802605200330774/1061712822423277668/Brown3.png"} size={"24px"}></TitleImage>100% chance of getting common<br />
<TitleImage image={"https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1040802605200330774/1061712822746234950/Green3.png"} size={"24px"}></TitleImage>25% + voting streak of getting uncommon<br />
<TitleImage image={"https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1040802605200330774/1061712823044018316/Red3.png"} size={"24px"}></TitleImage>5% + voting streak of getting rare<br />
<TitleImage image={"https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1040802605200330774/1061712822100300006/Purple3.png"} size={"24px"}></TitleImage>1% + voting streak of getting legendary<br />
<br />
You get 1 rare every 10 common/uncommon<br />
You get 1 legendary every 60 common/uncommon/rare<br />
