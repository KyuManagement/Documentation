What are lootboxes, how do I earn them and what are the odds?

<Underline />

Lootboxes are a way to gain a little exp boost.
You can earn Lootboxes as thanks by voting for the bot.<br />
You can open Lootboxes in every server that has the option to open them enabled.<br />

<br />
