King of the hill (KOTH) allows users to compete for the top spot.

<Underline />

King of the hill (KOTH) was inspired by the <Highlight>[AmariBot](https://amaribot.com)</Highlight> weekly system. Users are rewarded exp for their text messages, and the top users at the end of the event can be rewarded with badges, experience, and roles.
